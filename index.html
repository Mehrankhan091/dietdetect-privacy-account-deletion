DietDetect - Account Deletion

How to request deletion of your account and associated data

How to Request Account Deletion

  1. Open the DietDetect app on your device
  2. Go to Profile section (bottom navigation)
  3. Tap on "Settings" or "Account"
  4. Select "Delete Account"
  5. Confirm your decision by entering your password
  6. Tap "Delete Account" to complete the process

What Data Will Be Deleted

  • Your user account and profile information
  • All meal and nutrition data
  • Health goals and preferences
  • Onboarding information
  • App usage history
  • All stored images and food photos

Data Retention Policy

When you delete your account:

  • Immediate deletion: All personal data is removed from our servers
  • Backup deletion: Any backup data is deleted within 30 days
  • Analytics data: Anonymous usage statistics may be retained for app improvement
  • Legal compliance: Some data may be retained if required by law

  • Deletion will remove access to all DietDetect services
  • You can always create a new account later if needed
