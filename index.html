DietDetect - Account Deletion

How to request deletion of your account and associated data

To request deletion of your DietDetect account and associated data, please follow the appropriate procedure outlined below.

Delete via App
Delete via Email

How to Request Account Deletion via App

  1. Open the DietDetect app on your device
  2. Go to Profile section (bottom navigation)
  3. Tap on "Settings" or "Account"
  4. Select "Delete Account"
  5. Confirm your decision by entering your password
  6. Tap "Delete Account" to complete the process

Note: Using the in-app deletion process is the fastest and most secure way to delete your account, as it immediately verifies your identity.

Account Deletion Process via Email

  1. Compose an email from the email address associated with your DietDetect account
  2. Use the subject line: "DietDetect Account Deletion Request"
  3. Send the email to: privacy@dietdetect.example

Note: For verification purposes, we can only process deletion requests sent from the email address associated with your DietDetect account. Requests from other email addresses cannot be processed.

What Data Will Be Deleted

  • Your user account and profile information
  • All meal and nutrition data
  • Health goals and preferences
  • Onboarding information
  • App usage history
  • All stored images and food photos

Data Retention Policy

When you delete your account:

  • Immediate deletion: All personal data is removed from our active servers
  • Backup deletion: Any backup data is permanently deleted within 30 days
  • Analytics data: Anonymous usage statistics may be retained for app improvement
  • Legal compliance: Some data may be retained if required by law

What to Expect After Submission

  1. You will receive an automated confirmation email within 15 minutes of submitting your request
  2. Our team will process your request within 5-7 business days
  3. Once processed, you will receive a confirmation that your account and all associated data have been permanently deleted from our systems

Important Notes

  • Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone
  • Make sure to export any important data before deletion
  • Deletion will remove access to all DietDetect services
  • You can always create a new account later if needed

Need Assistance?

If you encounter any issues during this process or have additional questions, please contact our support team:

  • Email: support@dietdetect.example
  • In-App Support: Go to Profile → Help & Support
  • Response Time: We'll respond within 48 hours
